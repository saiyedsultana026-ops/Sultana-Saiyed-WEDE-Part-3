About Us

Evolve Tennis Academy is a tennis club located in Port Elizabeth and Johannesburg. It was founded by Danie Van Zyl in 2021. Danie has been a coach for the past 14 years. The academy is led by Danie Van Zyl along with his local and international expertise.

Danie Van Zyl worked as a head coach for the High Performance Tennis Academy (HPTA) and acting Killarney Country Club Tennis Director. Evolve Tennis Academy is well equipped to deliver structured individual and group lessons to both adults and kids.

The academy's main aim is to improve skills, emphasising tennis etiquette and sportsmanship. All coaches are fully qualified and SATA registered.

Vision

We aim to inspire and encourage players to evolve in their game and be the best they can be. The Evolve motto is "Every player can optimise his or her abilities!"

Mission

Our goal is to provide professional coaching while nurturing tennis player's individual talents in a safe, enjoyable and supportive environment. Our mission and vision reflects our core values: integrity and excellence, professionalism, care, fun and progress.

The simple fact is, we love tennis! Our amazing coaches really care about preparing you with the very best professional tennis coaching. We live tennis and push every player to improve to do whatever goals he or she has. We look forward to working with you and having you play at our prestige tennis courts. Everyone is welcome, all ages, all levels and aspirations.
